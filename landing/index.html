VIABUY Prepaid Mastercard with Apple Pay

Now you can pay with your VIABUY Mastercard using Apple Pay in stores, online and in apps. It's easy to use, secure and effortlessly mobile.

How to add your card

iPhone

On iPhone, open the Wallet app and tap the plus sign.

Apple Watch

On Apple Watch, open the Apple Watch app on your iPhone and select "Wallet & Apple Pay" then tap "Add Credit or Debit Card".

iPad

On iPad, go to Settings, open "Wallet & Apple Pay" and select "Add Credit or Debit Card".

MacBook with Touch ID

On MacBook with Touch ID, go to System Preferences and select "Wallet & Apple Pay" then select "Add Card".
